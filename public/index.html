About Gratis

Founded in 2017, Gratis is a data-driven platform for restaurants to eliminate food waste and move towards a profitable and sustainable path. Made by a team of recent college graduates, Gratis recognizes the hardships faced by students on campus with finding good, affordable and delicious food resources. Hence, Gratis users become food critics for our partnered restaurants in exchange of free food.
